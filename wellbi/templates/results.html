Syphillis is common in both men and women.

Common symptoms for Syphillis include painless sores, skin rashes, sores on body and/or in mouth/vagina/anus, fever, swollen lymph nodes, headaches or muscle aches, weight loss, fatigue, difficulty coordinating muscle movements, paralysis, numbness, dementia, and blindness.
