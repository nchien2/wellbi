From the symptoms you inputted it appears you may have Gonorrhea!

Gonorrhea is common in both men and women.

Common symptoms for females include cloudy/yellow vaginal discharge, abdominal or pelvic pain, irregular bleeding, and painful or more frequent urination.

Common symptoms for males include pus-like discharge from the tip of the penis, pain or swelling in one testicle, and pain or discomfort with urination.

We recommend you make an appointment to see a medical professional.
